Cheddar & Green Onion Gluten Free Biscuits

This recipe is gluten & egg free

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Bobs Red Mill all purpose flour
  • 1/2 a cup of almond flour
  • 2 tsp of baking powder
  • dash of salt
  • 1 cup, Plain Greek yogurt
  • 3/4 cup of grated cheddar
  • 1/3 cup of chopped green onion

Directions

Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees

  1. sift flour into a mixing bowl
  2. add baking powder and salt
  3. gently mix in yogurt
  4. fold in cheddar and green onions

The dough should be slightly sticky. Spoon onto non-stick baking sheet, shape as desired. Sprinkle a light layer of flour on top.


Bake for 10-13 mintues until golden on top.

Serve warm

Enjoy

this recipe is gluten free

Prawn Coconut Stir-fry

Ingredients

  • BC Spot Prawns
  • tsp coconut oil
  • Yellow peppers
  • onion
  • garlic
  • cilantro
  • mushrooms
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • red curry paste

Directions

  1. chop vegtables
  2. on medium heat saute garlic & onions in coconut oil
  3. add mushrooms & peppers
  4. stir in coconut milk and red curry paste
  5. add prawns and cook until they slightly curl

serve over rice

Enjoy

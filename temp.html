Roasted Red Pepper Spaghetti

Ingredients

Spaghetti Squash

  • olive oil
  • salt & pepper

Sauce

  • Ground turkey
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt & pepper
  • Shallots
  • Celery
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • 2 jars of roasted red peppers
  • tsp of sugar
  • White cheddar or cheese of your choice

Directions

Pre-heat oven to 375

  1. cut spaghetti squash in half length wise
  2. scoop out seeds and ribbing
  3. brush with olive oil and season with salt & pepper
  4. place cut side down on baking sheet & roast for 35 minutes or until tender
  5. let cool for several minutes
  6. scrape with fork to create spaghetti strands

While squash is roasting prepare sauce

  1. in the Foodie (or skillet) brown ground turkey on medium sear setting
  2. drain fat, add crushed garlic, italian seasoning, salt & pepper
  3. slice and diced shallots, celery and mushrooms
  4. add onion, celery, mushrooms and spinach
  5. blend roasted red peppers with tsp of sugar in a blender or food processor
  6. add roasted peppers and more seasoning as needed
  7. with a fork scrape spaghetti squash into sauce
  8. grate cheese and sprinkle ontop
  9. roast on 375 until the cheese is to your liking, approx 7-10 minutes
